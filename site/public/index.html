The motorcycle intercom already in your pocket.

Talk to your passenger — clearly, hands-free, and without buying €200 of intercom hardware. TwoUp connects two iPhones directly to each other: no internet, no SIM, no servers. Encrypted voice, phone to phone, with both phones locked in your pockets.

Join the TestFlight beta Requires two iPhones on iOS 26 or later, one per helmet.

Built for the ride

No internet. Anywhere.

Wi-Fi Aware links the phones directly. Tunnels, mountains, abroad without roaming — if the phones are within range of each other, you're talking.

Your headsets, not new ones

Works with the Bluetooth helmet headsets you already own — or any headset. The helmet's boom mic is used automatically.

Talk naturally

Full-duplex conversation with echo cancellation and noise suppression. No push-to-talk, no walkie-talkie turns.

Locked and pocketed

Pair once. The link connects automatically every ride, survives dropouts, and keeps running with both phones locked away.

Glove-friendly

One oversized mute button and an honest connection status you can read at a glance in a tank-bag mount.

Battery-friendly

A two-hour ride barely registers on either phone.

Private by design

Your voice never leaves the two phones — there are no servers for it to leave to. No account, no analytics, nothing collected, and the link is encrypted by iOS itself. The app is open source, so you don't have to take our word for it.

Free to try. Buy once, if you like it.

Every session is free for 15 minutes, restart as often as you like. One lifetime unlock removes the limit, and Family Sharing covers both of you with a single purchase. No subscription, ever.
